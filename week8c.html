Cambridge Inventors Club

Balloon Cars!

We'll be building cars powered by balloons.

You'll have a variety of materials to choose from, including different options for wheels. Build and iterate quickly to see what works best :)

We'll do two races, one short race to see if your car works at all, and one long race to see which car can go straight and fast best!
