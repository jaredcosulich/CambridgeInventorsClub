Pivotal Mentors Program

NoteCard Coding

Your mission: write paper-and- pencil “computer code” that lets anyone here recreate a basic drawing that you and your partner have drawn.

  1. You will be given an object to draw.
  2. Together, draw the item with pencil on paper.
  3. Together with your partner write a program that would allow any person to recreate your drawing exactly using "indexCard", a new programming language (see your handout).
  4. When everyone is done, we will rotate teams. Follow another team’s program, and draw their picture. Then, guess what object they were assigned (write it down and put it in their cup).

The team that has the most other teams guess their drawing will win!

Coding: Or code away on your own project! (let us know if you want help designing a project)
