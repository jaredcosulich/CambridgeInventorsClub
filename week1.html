Pivotal Mentors Program

The Goal

Get started coding (or get moving faster) such that you can continue making progress after the program ends.

This Week

We'll be coming around and talking with each of you to get to know you better.

While we do that we suggest exploring these three (very different) resources. We'd love to know which might be the best fit for you.

Codecademy Scratch CodeCombat
  • More traditional, lesson focused, but still very interactive and challenge-based.
  • Completely free with 25 million users and 12 programming languages supported
  • Drag and drop coding platform for building animations, games, etc.
  • Completely free, developed by MIT, large community of people sharing projects
  • Learn to code through a game. (you will explore very real programming languages and concepts)
  • Lots of free content covering wide range of topics, but also paid content.
Get Started:
  • Visit https://www.codecademy.com/
  • Fill out the registration form on the front page.
 Get Started:

 Get Started:
