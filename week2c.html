Cambridge Inventors Club

The Bird House Challenge

We'll be in small groups working to complete the challenge.

The Rules

  • Each team goes to a different conference room out of site of others.
  • Each team gets a 2'x2' square of tape on a table, with a tiny bird house and different shaped pebbles in the middle.
  • With unlimited rubber bands, sticks, tape, and cardstock, the teams need to build a way to get the pebbles into the birdhouse.
  • The team who can get the most pebbles in the birdhouse in 90 seconds wins.
  • Only one person can use only one hand (at a time), and no hand can cross into the 2x2 foot square.
  • Pebbles cannot come out of the square; the birdhouse cannot move at all.
